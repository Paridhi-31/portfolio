Paytm - SmartSplit

Simplifying Shared Expenses

The Automated Expense Splitting feature case study explores a strategic solution for Paytm to address the growing need for seamless shared expense management among its users. This feature targets the inconvenience users face when managing shared expenses, which often involves using third-party apps that complicate the payment process. By integrating an expense-splitting feature, Paytm aims to enhance user convenience by allowing shared expense tracking, customized splitting ratios, and automated reminders within the app, creating a unified financial experience.

Within this case study, readers will find:

  • An outline of Paytm's challenge in addressing shared expenses within its platform
  • Insights from market research and competitive analysis highlighting trends in digital payments and social financial transactions
  • User personas for target users such as social spenders and frequent app users
  • Product vision and structured feature ideation
  • A RICE-based prioritization of feature elements to maximize impact
