Spotify Ads 2.0

Revolutionizing User Engagement with Smart Ads

The Enhanced Ad Experience case study addresses Spotify’s challenge of balancing user satisfaction with effective ad delivery for advertisers. Free-tier users often experience frequent, irrelevant ads that disrupt their listening, while premium users question the value of their subscription if they still perceive branded content. To resolve these pain points, this solution proposes a refined ad strategy that prioritizes personalization, minimizes disruption, and introduces interactive formats that increase engagement without compromising user experience. By focusing on relevant ad delivery, Spotify can retain user trust, improve engagement, and boost advertiser ROI.

Within this case study, readers will find:

  • An overview of Spotify’s current ad delivery challenges across free and premium user bases
  • Market research and competitive analysis on personalized and non-intrusive ad trends
  • User personas illustrating free and premium user needs, alongside advertiser expectations
  • Product vision, goals, and structured solutions for personalized and interactive ad formats
  • Feature prioritization based on the RICE framework to maximize impact and minimize disruption
