Ola - Fuel Direct

Instant Fuel Access for Drivers’ Cash Flow

The Fuel Direct feature case study delves into a strategic solution designed to tackle high ride cancellation rates on the Ola platform by addressing a core operational challenge faced by drivers. Many drivers cancel rides due to cash flow concerns, especially when users select wallet payments, which restricts immediate access to funds for essential expenses like fuel. This feature aims to bridge this gap by offering drivers a reliable, instant source of funds specifically for fuel purchases, thereby empowering them to accept more rides without financial constraints. Through Fuel Direct, Ola seeks to improve ride reliability, enhance the user experience, and support drivers with innovative service tools that streamline their daily operations. The case study outlines how this feature can help Ola reduce cancellations, retain drivers, and meet user expectations for seamless, uninterrupted ride service.

Within this case study, readers will find:

  • An overview of Ola’s challenges with online payment-based cancellations
  • Market research and industry trends around instant earnings and fuel incentives
  • User personas representing drivers and riders affected by cancellations
  • Product vision, goals, and structured feature ideation
  • A RICE-based prioritization of feature elements to maximize impact
